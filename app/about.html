Long-term Gain.

MIEquity is a premier global asset management firm with $50 billion in assets under management across complementary private equity and credit businesses.

MIEquity pioneered a sector-focused approach to private equity investing with the vision that a dedicated team of industry experts could build exceptional companies of enduring value. Since the firm’s inception in 1989, MIEquity has invested in over 160 companies and become the leading equity investment firm focused on the media, communications, education and information industries.
